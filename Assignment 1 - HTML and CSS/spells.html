Spell Shaper Spell Designs

The custom spells created in “Spell Shaper” can have a large number of modifiers and effects added to them. Spell modifiers each have a unique “magic power” number which adds to the total magic power of the spell it is added to. The total magic power of a spell can not pass a certain number. The player may be able to bypass this through various means. Besides this, the only limit to the modification that can be done to a spell is the player’s creativity.

There are other planned spells besides “Fireball”, “Ice Spike”, and “Lightning”. Some ideas that may be included are:

  • Poison
  • Wind Blast
  • Time Control
  • Water Pump
  • General Buffs (speed, damage, etc.)
  • ULTIMA

The game is mostly randomized in which spells and modifiers are available to the player, but there will be some static spells and modifiers that are always given to the player at a specific time. These might be rewards for an enemy killed, something collected in a static location, etc.

An example order of spells and modifers collected could be:
(Bold = Static)

  1. SPELL: Fireball
  2. MOD: Three-way shot
  3. SPELL: Wind Blast
  4. MOD: Larger Projectile
  5. MOD: Faster Projectile
  6. SPELL: ULTIMA
  7. MOD: Five-way shot
